YSL Libre

Country: Italy
YSL Libre is a Floral fragrance for women, released in 2019. Created by perfumer Anne Flipo, it blends the floral notes of lavender and orange blossom with the richness of musk and vanilla. The fragrance opens with a fresh, aromatic lavender note, which is contrasted by the floral sweetness of orange blossom. The base is warm and sensual, with vanilla, cedarwood, and amber providing depth and richness. Libre is a bold and empowering fragrance, perfect for modern women who seek both freshness and intensity in their perfumes.
